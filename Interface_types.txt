# interface types

Layer 3 interfaces are the most common deployment. 

Tap interfaces are designed for span/mirroring.

Virtual wire also known as a bump in the wire are configured to permit traffic from an appliance to another through the firewall.

                        [  fw1  ]
                    eth0/1|   |eth0/2
[ sw1 ]---virtual wire---      --virtual wire--[sw2]
        virtual wire zone a   virtual wire zone b

Layer 2 interfaces are used for layer 2 traffic. You can also create layer2 zones to permit or drop.
